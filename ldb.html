Law Database

Summary

This database is designed for human rights lawyers in the MENA region. There is restricted access to this type of information, so this app was designed and creation with confidentiality as a top priority.

Organizing assumptions

The nature of this project requires confidentiality. The app is designed for lawyers and academics in the Middle East and North African region. With this obstacle in mind, the best first step is to gather the assumptions of the team working on it and the stakeholders.

User flow

Toronto Star Touch at this point had amassed a regular readership, but still had trouble with users not fully exploring the app. The app encourages interaction and playing around with screens and a lot of our user feedback indicated many users didn’t interact or tap things as much as we wanted.
