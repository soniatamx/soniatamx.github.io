Law Database

Summary

This database is designed for human rights lawyers in the MENA region. There is restricted access to this type of information, so this app was designed and creation with confidentiality as a top priority.

Organizing assumptions

The nature of this project requires confidentiality. The app is designed for lawyers and academics in the Middle East and North African region. With this obstacle in mind, the best first step is to gather the assumptions of the team working on it and the stakeholders.

User research

Due to the neccissity of confidentiality we were unable to interview users directly. Instead we opted to send a questionnaire with the goal of learning the main themes of their concerns around the app and how we could solve for it.

Synthesis

From our user research, we discovered that many of our target users (lawyers, students, scholars in the MENA region) were not familiar with the usage of online databases in their research process. Our goal was to make a simple database that would allow users to save articles or print them as needed. These were the key features needed for their work and we didn't want to add too much bloat with additional features.
