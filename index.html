UX Guidelines

The UX guidelines website was designed and developed for internal use for the Toronto Star Touch. Initially there was only a paper UX guidelines to refer to between departments. The UX director and I wanted to created an online repository to serve as a general set of rules for screen creation. So we started compiling screenshots and strong examples to use for consistent design.

Bees Matter

Bee's Matter was a daily special section insert in the Toronto Star Touch tablet app. The goal of the campaign for bees matter to drive readers to the bees matter program and to create advertisements for their free seeds program.
