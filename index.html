Nordstrom

Nordstrom collaborated with the Toronto Star to create daily custom content inserts in the Toronto Star Touch tablet apps. The screens are an amalgamation of written content, photographs, and illustrations to promote Nordstrom's opening at CF Eaton Centre and popup concepts. The screens covered their premium bar offerings, candy boutique, and stylist services.
