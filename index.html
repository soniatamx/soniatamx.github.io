Toronto Star Touch Promo Site

The Toronto Star Touch promo site redesign's goal was to highlight the app's key features and teach the users how to use the app. The home page was completely overhauled with a tutorial video as the hero. The how-to page outlines the app's features and benefits.

President's Choice Black Label

Nordstrom

Nordstrom collaborated with the Toronto Star to create daily custom content inserts in the Toronto Star Touch tablet apps. The screens are an amalgamation of written content, photographs, and illustrations to promote Nordstrom's opening at CF Eaton Centre and popup concepts. The screens covered their premium bar offerings, candy boutique, and stylist services.

UX Guidelines

The UX guidelines website was designed and developed for internal use for the Toronto Star Touch. Initially there was only a paper UX guidelines to refer to between departments. The UX director and I wanted to created an online repository to serve as a general set of rules for screen creation. So we started compiling screenshots and strong examples to use for consistent design.

Tangerine Basketball Journeys

Tangerine Basketball Journeys is a partnered special section with Toronto Star Touch. It received the 2017 Great Idea Award for Best Digital Innovation in the Daily Newspaper class from News Media Canada. During its runtime, this special section was a daily insert in the Star Touch. For the purpose of the showcase, it has been combined into one edition.

Auto Show 2017

The 2017 Auto Show was a partnered special section daily edition with the Toronto Star Touch. The editions had editorial content and ads that would showcase different cars and encourage readers to attend the show. Every edition featured a Car of the Day.

The Toronto Star App Launch

The Toronto Star App website was wireframed using Photoshop, Sketch and Zeplin.io. A quick wireframe was needed for the pre-launch and post-launch of the Toronto Star mobile and tablet app. The homepage takeover had to feature Toronto Star's iconic chevrons and bright blue, clearly state that it was releasing a new app separate from the Star Touch, explain to the users that it would be available for both tablet and smartphone and briefly outline its features.
