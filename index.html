Streamlined App Registration

Goals: Redesign the app registration to get users to the app quickly, and effortlessly, while retaining users through the email verification process.

Registration can be a very frustrating experience to users as it is the main barrier of entry to an app. It was important to me to establish trust in the app, and to get users through the form filling as efficiently as possible.
